Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 40oz tomato sauce (meat optional)
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350F. Cook pasta al dente.
  2. Heat up pasta sauce and stir in any additional seasonings.
  3. Add 1 cup of tomato sauce to large pan, preferrably 9x13
  4. Layer with 1/3 cheese mixture and 1 cup of meat sauce. Repeat twice more
  5. Finish with 3 noodles topped with remaining sauce.
  6. Cover pan with foil and bake 45 minutes
  7. Remove foil and sprinkle the remaining Mozarella and Parmesan cheeses.
  8. Bake for 15 more minutes.
  9. Let cool for 10-15 minutes.
  10. Enjoy!
